Getting Started with Crypto

Learn the fundamentals of cryptocurrency and blockchain technology.

What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual form of currency that uses cryptography for security. Unlike traditional currencies, cryptocurrencies are decentralized systems based on blockchain technology.

Understanding Blockchain

Blockchain is a distributed ledger technology that records all transactions across a network of computers. It's transparent, secure, and immutable.

Types of Digital Assets

Digital assets include cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum), tokens (utility, security), and NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens).

Security Best Practices

Protect your investments using hardware wallets, strong passwords, two-factor authentication, and being aware of common scams.

Crypto Terms

Blockchain

A digital ledger of transactions maintained across a network of computers.

Mining

The process of validating and recording cryptocurrency transactions.

Wallet

Digital storage for cryptocurrency assets and private keys.
